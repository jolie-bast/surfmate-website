What is Surfmate?

Surfmate is a surf app for capturing your surf moments — not just your sessions. It's a place to keep what really matters to you in surfing. Not performance. Not comparison. But moments, emotions, and the journey behind them.

It's about remembering why you paddle out — again and again.

Surfmate helps you document your time in the water in a way that feels personal. Log what matters to you: surf sessions and spots, notes, photos, conditions, emotions, people, memories. You decide.
