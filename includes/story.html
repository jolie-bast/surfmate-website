The Story

There was a girl with a dream. A dream of becoming a surfer.

After her first surf session, she tattooed a wave on her back - a promise to herself that this would become her life.

But becoming a surfer was not easy.

No surf friends. No surf community. No idea where to start.

So she figured it out herself: solo surf trips, wrong spots, missed conditions, chasing waves everywhere.

Over time, surfing became part of her identity.

But over time, the journey got lost - disappeared in camera rolls, disappearing stories, and fading memories.

That is why Surfmate exists.

To remember every surf.

To stay connected.

To build your own surf story.

Your surf. Your story.
