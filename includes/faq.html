Sessions

Surfmate helps you capture every surf that matters. You can log:

  • Surf spot, date and time
  • Session duration and overall rating
  • Up to 3 photos and your notes
  • Surf conditions, emotions and vibes
  • Session highlights

You decide. Sessions can stay completely private or be visible to your friends.

Nothing gets exposed. Sessions at secret spots are currently always private, even for your followers. Your spots stay yours.

Surfmate helps you remember:

  • where you surfed
  • how the conditions were
  • how the session felt
  • who you surfed with
  • how your surf journey evolves over time

It is your personal surf history.

Forecast integrations are planned for future versions.

Spotchecks

A Spotcheck is a quick photo or up to 20-second video from a surf spot that stays visible for 24 hours.

It lets your friends instantly see what the ocean looks like right now, without sharing the same videos across group chats.

Everyone who follows you can see your Spotchecks. They can also enable push notifications to get alerted when you post one.

Spotchecks stay visible for 24 hours. Your own past Spotchecks remain saved in your personal archive.

Surf conditions change fast. Instead of sending the same update to multiple chats, post one Spotcheck and everyone instantly knows what is going on.

Cleaner, faster, and built for surfing.

Yes. If a Spotcheck is posted at a secret spot, only people who know that spot can see it.

Yes. You can enable push notifications for the surfers you follow and instantly know when they check the waves.

Following

Following keeps you connected to your surf crew. You see their Spotchecks and know when conditions are good, where people are surfing, and when friends are checking the waves.

You can:

  • Scan their Surfmate QR code
  • Share profile links
  • Search by username
  • Search in your contacts friends and invite them

Invite your surf crew and stay connected on and off the water.
